Pythagorean Expectation

Baseball
Realized win and expected win differentials
Author

Miguel Malabayabas

Published

November 25, 2025

Pythagorean expectation estimates how many games a team should have won based on its runs scored and allowed. This chart shows the difference between each team’s realized wins and expected wins during the 2025 MLB regular season.

Key Insights:

  • The Cleveland Guardians, Miami Marlins, and Los Angeles Angels outperformed their Pythagorean expectations by eight wins.
  • The Texas Rangers and Chicago White Sox fell short, losing ten more games than expected.
  • Surprisingly, the World Series champion Los Angeles Dodgers finished the regular season with three fewer wins than expected.

Data: 2025 MLB Regular Season Standings
